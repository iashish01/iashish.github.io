The National Jute Board (NJB) of India is an autonomous body established under the Ministry of Textiles, Government of India. Its primary objective is to promote the jute industry and improve the production, development, and marketing of jute products. The NJB plays a crucial role in the economic upliftment of jute farmers and the overall growth of the jute sector in India.

Key Features:

  • Promotion of Jute Cultivation: Encourages farmers to cultivate jute through various initiatives and support programs.
  • Research and Development: Supports R&D activities to enhance jute production techniques and develop new jute products.
  • Marketing Support: Facilitates marketing of jute products by organizing trade fairs, exhibitions, and buyer-seller meets.
  • Skill Development: Provides training and skill development programs for workers and entrepreneurs in the jute sector.
  • Policy Formulation: Advises the government on policy matters related to the jute industry and ensures the implementation of various schemes.
  • Financial Assistance: Offers financial assistance and subsidies to promote the jute industry and support farmers.
